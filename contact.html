Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a reservation?

Walk-ins are always welcome! However, we recommend reservations for our meditation sessions, workshops, and if you're planning to visit during peak hours (weekends 2-5 PM).

Can I work on my laptop?

We have a designated quiet work area with WiFi, but we encourage you to use it mindfully. Other zones are screen-free to maintain the peaceful atmosphere.

Do you host private events?

Yes! We host book clubs, meditation groups, small workshops, and intimate gatherings. Contact us to discuss your event needs and availability.

Are you child-friendly?

We welcome families! We ask that children respect the quiet atmosphere. We have a small children's book corner and coloring supplies available.
